About Me

Hi! My name is Marcelo, and this is my website. I've lived in San Francisco my whole life, but I'll be moving across the country in August, when college begins.

This website is a repository with big windows. Certain pages (the Media Archive) are primarily for my own use, while others (Photography) only exist for others to see. I've kept the About Me proper as short as possible. I hope my site tells you more about me than these words.

This website will never be finished. I'll be adding things in a slow stream as I stumble across new interests and technical issues, so feel free to check back in at any point to see what (if anything) has changed.

Have fun!
